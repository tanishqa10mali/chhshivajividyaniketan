About Our School's Academic Focus

Additional Miscellaneous Fee Details

An additional miscellaneous fee of Rs. 3000/- must be deposited along with the primary food and accommodation fee.

This fee covers the School Uniform, Sports Uniform, and Nightwear. It also includes charges for Medical/Doctor visits, Laundry, and Haircuts. Necessary personal materials will be provided as needed. If the expenses exceed Rs. 3000/-, the remaining extra balance must be paid by the parents.
