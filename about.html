Chairman's Message

Respected Wise Parents,

A good thought can shape a child’s future… “Investment in Knowledge Pays the Best Interest.”
An investment made in knowledge gives the best return.

A parent’s true wealth cannot be measured in money, property, or material possessions. The real treasure of every mother and father is their child. Today’s students are tomorrow’s citizens and the true wealth of our nation.

Therefore, considering students as the central focus and aiming at their all-round personality development, Chhatrapati Shivaji (Military Pattern Residential) Vidyaniketan, Peth has been established.

In today’s materialistic environment, for the healthy growth, character building, and overall development of students, our campus is always committed and dedicated.

“We do not give admission based on quality; we create quality after giving admission.”

We do not just shape a student’s future; we prepare students for the future. To strengthen students physically, mentally, and emotionally, and to turn their dreams into reality, we provide an excellent environment for holistic development — along with science and technology, yet away from excessive mobile and television distractions.

We invite you to confidently secure your child’s admission in our educational campus, which works tirelessly for the overall development of students.
